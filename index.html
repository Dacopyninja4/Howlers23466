About Us

Welcome to the official website for The Howlers! We are Team 23466 and we operate in Edison, NJ. We have been doing so since joining the First Tech Challenge as an FTC team in 2022. The Howlers are a dynamic robotics team dedicated to innovation and excellence in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Comprising of students with diverse skills, we collaborate to design and program our robots, demonstrating our commitment to teamwork and creativity.

Our spirit of collaboration shines as we face challenges, embodying sportsmanship while inspiring our community to embrace science and technology.
