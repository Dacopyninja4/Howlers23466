Who are we?

The Howlers are an energetic robotics team competing in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Comprising students with diverse skills, we collaborate to design and program our robots each season.

Our teamwork and creativity shine as we tackle challenges on the field. By supporting one another in our various roles, we exemplify sportsmanship and inspire our community to embrace science and technology.
